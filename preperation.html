Session Preparation Guide

Before Your Session

  • Stay well hydrated in the 24 hours prior to your session.
  • Eat a light, nourishing meal before your visit.
  • Wear comfortable clothing—layers are best.
  • Feel free to bring a book, journal, or anything that supports your relaxation.

During Your Session

  • You will be seated or reclining in a peaceful environment.
  • Light music or meditative silence will accompany your session.
  • Allow yourself to relax deeply—some people fall asleep, others meditate or simply rest.

After Your Session

  • Drink extra water to assist with detoxification.
  • Consider taking a sea salt bath or foot soak to continue the cleansing process.
  • Avoid stimulants like caffeine and alcohol for several hours if possible.
  • Be gentle with yourself—rest, walk in nature, or journal your experience.
