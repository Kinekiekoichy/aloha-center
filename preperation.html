Before Your Session

  • Stay well hydrated—drink plenty of water the day before and day of your session.
  • Wear comfortable, relaxed clothing.
  • Avoid heavy meals or stimulants such as caffeine right before your visit.
  • Arrive a few minutes early to center yourself and prepare mentally for healing.

During Your Session

  • You’ll be seated or reclining in a serene, energy-enhanced environment.
  • We recommend closing your eyes and focusing on your breath or meditative thoughts.
  • Some people sleep, others enter deep meditative states—go with what feels natural.

After Your Session

  • Continue drinking water to support detoxification.
  • Rest if needed. Your body may be processing and realigning energy.
  • Consider taking a sea salt bath or foot soak to aid energetic clearing.
  • Listen to your body and nourish it with healthy food and sleep.
